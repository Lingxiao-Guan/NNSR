NERCN NeuroSignal Research (NNSR) 平台

NERCN NeuroSignal Research (NNSR) Platform

先进的深部脑信号处理与分析平台

Advanced Deep Brain Signal Processing & Analysis Platform

NNSR专为处理和分析神经信号而设计，提供全面的工具集，助力深部脑刺激及神经科学研究突破。

NNSR is designed for processing and analyzing neural signals, offering comprehensive tools for deep brain stimulation and breakthroughs in neuroscience research.

核心功能

Key Features

智能信号处理

Intelligent Signal Processing

基于神经网络的伪迹检测系统，确保信号质量与研究可重复性。通过深度学习算法，自动识别和处理异常信号，提高数据可靠性。

Neural network-based artifact detection system, ensuring signal quality and research reproducibility. Automatically identifies and processes abnormal signals through deep learning algorithms, improving data reliability.

高级可视化

Advanced Visualization

直观呈现复杂神经动态模式，支持时间、频率和时频分析。

Intuitive representation of complex neural dynamics, supporting time, frequency and time-frequency analyses.

实时处理

Real-time Processing

高性能实时信号处理，支持在线分析与特征提取，延迟最小。

High-performance real-time signal processing, supporting online analysis and feature extraction with minimal latency.

闭环研究平台

Closed-Loop Platform

支持复杂刺激-响应研究，实现交互式神经调节与定制控制机制。完美集成硬件与软件系统，为神经调控研究提供一站式解决方案。

Supporting complex stimulation-response research with interactive neuromodulation and customizable control mechanisms. Seamlessly integrates hardware and software systems, providing an all-in-one solution for neuromodulation research.
